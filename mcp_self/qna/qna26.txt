What is the 'Paperclip Maximizer' thought experiment about?
A hypothetical AI with a single goal of maximizing paperclips, which might eventually decide to eliminate humans to convert their atoms into paperclips, demonstrating unintended and extreme consequences of narrow goal-setting

What is the 'Paperclip Golden Retriever' effect in AI and agent behavior?
An agent or AI system that follows instructions literally and without regard for collateral damage, potentially destroying existing systems or data to accomplish its given task

What security precaution did the team implement for MCP servers regarding data manipulation?
They added guardrails that prompt users before dropping data, with instructions like 'unless prompted by the user specifically, please don't drop data' and always asking for confirmation

What is the recommended approach for future agent access and authentication?
Just-in-time (JIT) access, where agents request specific, time-bounded permissions for individual tasks and then lose access after completing the task

What supply chain security concerns should be considered with AI agents?
Potential risks include compromised servers, bad employee relationships, accidental uploads, service downtime, and unauthorized data exfiltration
